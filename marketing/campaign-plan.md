# Edunostics Marketing Campaign Plan

## Positioning

Edunostics is an all-in-one academic operations and report card system for Nigerian schools. It replaces scattered spreadsheets, manual result formatting, disconnected attendance records, and delayed report publishing with one controlled school dashboard.

## Core Promise

Run scores, broadsheets, report cards, attendance, assignments, quizzes, lesson plans, behaviour ratings, and parent-ready results from one platform.

## Campaign Theme

**Stop managing school results with scattered spreadsheets.**

Supporting message:

**Edunostics gives school owners, administrators, and teachers one reliable workflow for scores, report cards, broadsheets, attendance, and academic planning.**

## Primary Audiences

### Proprietors and School Owners

Pain points:
- Weak visibility into academic workflow.
- Reports delayed until the end of term.
- Teacher coverage and attendance gaps are discovered too late.
- Heavy dependence on one person managing spreadsheets.

Message:
See every class, score workflow, attendance gap, teacher coverage issue, and unpublished report from one dashboard.

Proof screens:
- Dashboard
- Insights
- Priority alerts
- Recent activity
- Role access management

### School Administrators

Pain points:
- Manual result setup every term.
- Report card formatting stress.
- Difficult class, subject, teacher, and grading configuration.
- Hard to manage academic sessions and term dates cleanly.

Message:
Configure sessions, classes, subjects, grading rules, report templates, school branding, and staff access once.

Proof screens:
- Students
- Teachers
- Classes
- Subjects
- Settings
- Report Card Templates
- Broadsheet Templates

### Teachers

Pain points:
- Repetitive score entry.
- Manual assignment and quiz workflow.
- Scheme of work tracking.
- Little visibility into class progress.

Message:
Enter scores, submit for review, create lessons, publish quizzes, assign work, and track class progress without spreadsheets.

Proof screens:
- Score Entry
- Score Reviews
- Class Progress
- Lessons
- Quizzes
- Assignments
- Scheme of Work

### Parents and Students

Pain points:
- Late access to results.
- Unclear progress reporting.
- Paper-only academic communication.

Message:
Give parents and students easier access to published results, assignments, quizzes, and academic progress.

Proof screens:
- Report card preview
- Published report workflow
- Assignments
- Quizzes
- Communication

## Campaign Offer

### Primary Offer

**Free School Result Workflow Audit**

Offer copy:

Send us your current result process. We will show how Edunostics can reduce manual work across score entry, broadsheets, report cards, attendance, and parent publishing.

### Secondary Offer

**Book a 15-minute demo**

Use this after the user has seen screenshots or clicked an ad.

## Funnel

1. Awareness
   - Facebook and Instagram screenshot ads.
   - WhatsApp broadcast to school owners and administrators.
   - Short LinkedIn posts for proprietors and edtech buyers.

2. Interest
   - Landing page with real app screenshots.
   - Feature carousels: dashboard, score entry, report cards, broadsheet, attendance, lessons.
   - WhatsApp follow-up with 2-minute screen recording.

3. Conversion
   - Free workflow audit.
   - Demo booking.
   - School setup consultation.

4. Close
   - Show the school their current pain points mapped to Edunostics modules.
   - Offer onboarding for current session or next term.

## Main Campaign Assets

1. Hero screenshot ad: dashboard with alerts.
2. Score workflow ad: score entry and score review.
3. Report card ad: branded report preview and PDF workflow.
4. Broadsheet ad: broadsheet preview and download PDF.
5. Academic tools ad: lessons, quizzes, assignments, scheme of work.
6. School operations ad: students, teachers, classes, subjects, role access.

## Success Metrics

- WhatsApp replies from school decision makers.
- Demo bookings.
- Landing page conversion rate.
- Cost per qualified lead.
- Number of schools requesting workflow audit.
- Number of schools onboarded per term.

## 30-Day Rollout

Week 1:
- Publish landing page campaign copy.
- Prepare screenshot creatives.
- Send first WhatsApp broadcast.
- Run small Facebook/Instagram awareness campaign.

Week 2:
- Retarget people who engaged with screenshot ads.
- Share report-card and broadsheet proof posts.
- Start demo outreach to warm leads.

Week 3:
- Push workflow audit offer.
- Post teacher-focused content around score entry, lessons, quizzes, and assignments.
- Collect objections from prospects.

Week 4:
- Run conversion ads.
- Share before/after workflow posts.
- Follow up with all warm leads before term-end reporting pressure.
