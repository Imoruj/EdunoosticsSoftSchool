# Creative Brief

## Visual Direction

Use real product screenshots wherever possible. The strongest marketing angle is product proof: clean dashboard UI, Nigerian school workflow, report card previews, broadsheets, score entry, and settings.

Keep creatives simple:

- One main screenshot.
- One short headline.
- One supporting line.
- One CTA.
- Blue primary action color.
- White or light grey background.
- Avoid clutter and too many screenshots per creative.

## Brand Style

Palette:
- Primary blue: #2563EB or #1E40AF.
- Success green: #16A34A.
- Neutral dark: #111827.
- Soft background: #F8FAFC.
- Warning amber: #F59E0B.

Typography:
- Use a clean sans-serif similar to Inter.
- Keep headlines bold and short.
- Use sentence case for supporting copy.
