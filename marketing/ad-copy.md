## LinkedIn Posts

### Post 1

Most schools do not have a result problem. They have a workflow problem.

Scores are entered in one file. Broadsheets are formatted somewhere else. Comments are collected manually. Attendance is separate. Report cards are rushed at the end of term.

Edunostics brings the full academic workflow into one dashboard: score entry, review, broadsheets, report cards, attendance, lessons, assignments, quizzes, and school settings.

Built for Nigerian schools that want a cleaner result cycle.

### Post 2

For school proprietors, the key question is not only "Are report cards ready?"

It is:
- Which classes have incomplete scores?
- Which reports are still unpublished?
- Which subjects have teacher coverage gaps?
- Which attendance records are missing?
- Which students need academic attention?

Edunostics gives school leadership visibility before the end-of-term rush.

### Post 3

Report-card automation should not remove school identity.

Edunostics supports school logo, signatures, grading rules, behaviour ratings, psychomotor skills, comments, attendance, academic performance tables, and branded PDF report layouts.

The result is faster processing without losing the school's official format.
